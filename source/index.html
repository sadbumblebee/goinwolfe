Full Belly Farms

The Venue

Since Dana's days studying at Berkeley, she's enjoyed the fresh fruit and veggies from Full Belly Farms. Every wednesday we pick up our CSA from up the street. And nearly every morning, Dana's on Instagram looking at pictures any number of the farm's baby goats, chickens, or cows. When we realized we could rent their new barn for our wedding, we didn't hesitate.

We'll be exchanging vows in the seclusion of the farm's walnut orchard. Then we'll join the party back at barn for drinks and lawn games. The setting sun on the valley will play backdrop to a family style three course meal made with seasonal veggies. Late into an evening of dancing, we'll depart to camp under the stars.
